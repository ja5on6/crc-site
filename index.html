Personal Profile

I recently came across The Cloud Resume Challenge by Forrest Brazeal and thought it's a pretty cool side project. To have the certifications and knowledge is one thing, but to be able to hands on and do is another level.

To go through my own "baptism by fire", I got started and created this completely serverless site.
